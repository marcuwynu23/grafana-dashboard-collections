# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive.
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments.
- Public or private discrimination.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may take corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private communication channel with maintainers whenever possible. Reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly and fairly.
